Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start an exam? +

Navigate to the Dashboard, select the exam you wish to take from the 'Available Exams' list, and click the 'Start Exam' button. Review the instructions before beginning.

Can I review my answers after submitting? +

Yes, immediately after submitting, you are redirected to the results page, where you can see a breakdown of your score and a detailed review of all questions, including correct answers and your selected options.

How do I reset my password? +

Go to the 'Profile' section, then select 'Security & Privacy'. You will find a button there to initiate the password change process.
