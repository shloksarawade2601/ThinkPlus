Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +

You can reset your password from the 'Security & Privacy' section of your profile settings. Click the 'Change Password' button and follow the on-screen instructions.

Where can I view my billing history? +

Billing information and payment history are available in the 'Subscription' area of the main dashboard. Look for the 'Manage Subscription' link.

How long does a support response usually take? +

Our support team aims to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, Monday through Friday. Critical issues may be addressed faster.

Can I change my primary email address? +

Yes, you can update your email address in the 'Personal Information' section of your profile. A confirmation link will be sent to the new email address.
